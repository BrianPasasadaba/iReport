REPORT AN INCIDENT

The CDRRMO is responsible for overseeing disaster risk management programs within its jurisdiction, coordinating activities to meet national standards, conducting risk assessments, and creating Local Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Plans (LDRRMPs) in collaboration with local councils. It operates early warning systems, monitors disasters, and mobilizes resources during emergencies while fostering partnerships with various stakeholders.

Individuals can promptly report emergencies like fires, traffic accidents, and rescue situations, supplementing their reports with photos taken directly from the app to ensure accuracy. Geotagging provides precise location data for swift response, while detailed incident descriptions empower authorities to allocate resources effectively. i-Report facilitates active citizen engagement in community safety, fostering collaboration between residents and emergency responders to build resilient, responsive cities.
