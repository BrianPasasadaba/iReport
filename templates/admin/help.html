Frequently Asked Questions

Change the status into Ongoing:

Step 1: Click the file icon in the View Details column.

Step 2: Locate and click the "Edit" button.

Step 3: Locate the status dropdown and change it into Ongoing.


Change the status into Resolved:

Step 1: Click the file icon in the View Details column.

Step 2: Locate and click the "Edit" button.

Step 3: Fill out the incident details field with information from the rescue team.

Step 4: Locate the status dropdown and change it into Resolved.

An account cannot be personally and physically edited by the account holder. They must contact office's IT department to update any information about their account.
