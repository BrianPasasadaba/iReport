FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

There are two ways to report an incident:


Using the hotline:

Step 1: Call the hotline at +49 508 1159.

Step 2: Describe the incident to the operator. The more details you provide, the better they can assist you

Step 3: Wait for the first responders and further instructions.


Using i-Report

Step 1: Click the "Report an Incident" button.

Step 2: Fill out all the necessary details including contact number, location,

number of victims, incident photo, and further details. You can also share your

GPS location by clicking the checkbox beside 'Share my Location'.

Step 3: Once done, click "Submit" button and wait for the first responders.

The Cabuyao City Disaster Risk and Reduction and Management Office is located at the Cabuyao Retail Plaza, Cabuyao City, Laguna.
